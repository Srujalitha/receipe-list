Aloo Paratha

Ingridients

  • 2 large potatoes peeled
  • 3cups of flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp ghee

    • Directions

      1. Make the dough ready. 2. Knead it for 10 to 15 minutes until it smooth.
      3. Cover the bowl with tea towel, and rest it for 30minutes.
      4. For filling, boil the potatoes in salted water until cooked through.
      5. Mash the potatoes and add all ingridients except ghee.
      6. Divide the dough into four equal parts and roll them into tight balls.
      7. Place an amount of potato mixture that is equal to or slightly more than the dough you
      started with in the centre.
      8. Bring the sides of the dough around the potato ball and close tightly.
      9. Flip the ball so the seam is on the bottom and roll out until it's about the size of the small plate,
      trying not break the dough or let the potato mixture escape.
      10. Heat the tawa over medium - high heat, add a teaspoon of ghee, and cook the flat bread until golden brown on
      both sides.
