Simple Omelette Receipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette beaten eggs
cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables
or meat.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximately 10min
  • Prepartion:5minutes
  • Cooking:5minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings:diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In abowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are
    well mixed. you can add a tbsp of water or milk for a fluffer texture.
  2. Heat the pan:Place a non stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. Tilt the
    pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings:When the eggs begin to set at edges but are still slightly
    runny in the middle, sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Fold and serve:As the omelette continues to coook, carefully lift one edge and fold it
    over the fillings. Let it cook for another minute, then slide into plate.
  6. Enjoy:Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
